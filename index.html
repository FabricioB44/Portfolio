About me

Hi, I'm me.

But you already know that, onto other things about me. That's me in the picture. I was born, raised and currently reside in the city of Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Since my family brought a work computer home when I was five years old, I've always been fascinated by anything related to those pixels on-screen. From games, then the internet, to hardware, operating systems, user interface and so on. All of this naturally led to me pursuing a software career.

Software engineering to be exact, due to it's diversity of roles within the industry, which is really nice because I'm young, gaining experience and still trying to find my calling. I'm eager to learn more about other programming languages and new tricks from the ones I already know.

My
education

Learning everyday.

After obtaining a Communication-Oriented Bachelors diploma in high school, I enrolled in a variety of other schools and courses.
